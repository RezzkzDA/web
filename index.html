How to Connect

Follow these simple steps to join our server:

  1. Open Minecraft.
  2. Click on "Multiplayer."
  3. Click on "Add Server."
  4. Enter the server name and IP address.
  5. Click "Done" and select the server from the list.
  6. Click "Join Server" to start playing!
